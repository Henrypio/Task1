ALBERT EINSTEIN

Theoretical physicist:


Albert Einstein was born in 1879 in Germany, the first child of

a bourgeois Jewish couple. The young Albert displayed an

early interest in science, but he was unhappy with the principles of obedience

and conformity that governed his Catholic elementary school.


Albert Einstein's favorite quote is "Imagination is more important than knowledge.

Knowledge is limited. Imagination encircles the world."
